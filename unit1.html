Easy Homemade Lasagna Recipe

This lasagna recipe is super easy with simple ingedients that anybody will be able to make it! With amazing flavor everybody in the family will be sure to enjoy.

Ingredients:

  • Ricotta cheese
  • Spices
  • Ground beef
  • Pausta sauce
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan
  • Mozzarella

How to make Lasagna

  • Boil pasta

    Cook in a large pot of salted water

  • Prepare meat sauce

    Cook beef, drain well, and then add pasta sauce and spices

  • Combine cheese mixture

    Mix the parmesan and the ricotta cheese

  • Layer and bake

    Layer the meat sause and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until golden brown.

How to layer Lasagna

  1. Spread a cup of meat sauce into a 9/13 in pan and add a layer of noodles
  2. Top the noodles with some of the cheese mixture, repeat the layers ending with a layer of noodles and sauce
  3. Cover with foil and bake. Remove foil and top with mozzarella and parmesan, then bake for another 15 minutes.

How long to Bake


This lasagna bakes for a total of one hour but to get it the perfect golden brown, you'll need to bake it in two stages.Then let the lasagna rest for at least 15 minutes.

This recipe is great the first time but it's also great to freeze so you can have it for a quick meal.
